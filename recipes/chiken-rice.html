Rice with chiken(aka mohamed elhadi's rice)

Description:

This is a faster, and less expensive rice.(as i say )

Fast, Easy to make rice and chiken plate.

Ingredients:

  • 100g (uncoocked) rice
  • 500g (raw) chicken breast
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • maybe some curcum for that delicous yellow color ?
  • 20ml of olive oil(or vegetable oil if you cant)

Steps:

  1. Wach that dirty chiken, wach it good
  2. Cut in into small pieces, add salt and peper
  3. Get a good pan, Pull some fire, add oilve oil, and let it heats (dont let it burns thought !)
  4. Put rice in a medium bool.
  5. pour 3/4 cups of water in that boil, make a strong fire, let it till it boil's
  6. When the water is boiling, release some fire, and cover that boild .
  7. Add salt,peper, and curcum(if wanter, but it's highly reccommanded !)
  8. Let in on petit feux pour 10~15 min.
  9. When coocked, add dat chiken, mix good !
  10. Voila ! enjoy (ps: dont forget to share with a another human being, or a cat, if u have one !)
